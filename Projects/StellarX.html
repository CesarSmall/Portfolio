StellarX

a no-code, AI-enabled XR platform developed by OVA that allows users to create and experience immersive virtual and mixed reality simulations.

What was the project?

StellarX is a VR based platform that allows for users to create simulations of environments without needing to know how to code in an easy to use, user-friendly sandbox environment. It can be used for training simulations, maintenance applications, education and more.

My contributions to the project

During my time as an intern working on StellarX, I was responsible for implementing the analytics system for the project.
This included the implementtion of systems like Google Analytics.

What I learned from my time there

During my time at OVA I learned not only how to navigate larger databases but I feel like one of the most fundamental things that was taught was how to not get overwhelmed in the face of bigger scaled projects and deadlines. Learning to navigate such a large codebase and read through and identify/read code was challenging at first but eventually became second nature. Understanding how to manage expectations and deadlines in a healthy way was also part of the adventure and I am glad that I was able to experience it with such a talented team.
