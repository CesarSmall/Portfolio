The Signal

My first big project that I got to work on.

What is The Signal?

The Signal is a sci-fi open world survival craft game that I worked on as a Junior Gameplay Programmer and as a UI programmer at Goosebyte Studios.

The game is set on an alien planet where the player must scavenge for resources, build shelter, and fend off hostile creatures while uncovering the mystery behind a strange signal that beckoned them to come to their planet to find their lost relative.

The project was announced to the public in mid-2023 via IGN and has frequent playtests and updates.
Link to the IGN article

Since then, the game has changed drastically, even including a rename to The Signal: Stranded On Sirenis and a complete art overhaul.

My contributions to the project

During my time working on The Signal, I was responsible for implementing various gameplay mechanics and systems, including but not limited to:

  • Player movement and controls
  • Inventory management system
  • Crafting system
  • UI elements such as health bars, inventory screens, and crafting menus
Given that we were a small team I was involved in many parts of the development of the game. I covered gameplay, UI, UX and managed a small team for the UI.

Working on The Signal was a great learning experience for me as it allowed me to apply my programming skills in a real-world setting and collaborate with other developers, artists, and designers to bring the game to life.

My Aspirations

While I don't think I'll be the next Notch or anything, I am aiming to hone my skills as well as possible to become as good of a developer that I can and I hope to one day be able to proudly say that I'm either part of or have developed something with great meaning to a lot of people.

I simply want to be able to have a positive impact on the world through my work, no matter how big or small that impact may be.
